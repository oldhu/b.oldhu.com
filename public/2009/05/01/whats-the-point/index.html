What's the Point?

May 1, 2009

I'm not going to ask questions about meanings of life/work/blahblah... I'm going to quote some lines from Boston Legal.

> Denny Crane: Edwin Poole's problem is he doesn't like being Edwin Poole(Edwin is a named partner and a psycho). From time to time he'd look in the mirror and ask, "What's the point?" I never do that. Questions like that'll kill you.

> Alan Shore: Questions like, "What's the point?"

> Denny Crane: ...... What's the point? You don't ask -- that's the point. You gonna win, by the way? The world wants to know.
